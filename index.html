I'm a product marketer who lives at the intersection of complex financial products and the buyers who need them. My background is a bit unusual — I studied Psychology and Biology with a concentration in Marketing Psychology, which means I think a lot about how people make decisions, not just what they decide.

At Wisetack, I've owned go-to-market strategy for a standalone lending platform serving 20,000+ merchants. That's meant everything from writing the messaging frameworks and battlecards that help sales win deals, to personally building the feature launch pages, partner enablement playbooks, and email campaigns that activate and retain merchants.

I'm a builder. I don't just set strategy and hand things off — I'm the one writing the copy, running the A/B tests, and staying close to the data to know what's actually working. If you want someone who can think at the strategic level and still produce high-quality assets themselves, that's me.
